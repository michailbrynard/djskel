Objectives:

Thank you for participating in this GSMA M4D Impact VAS Survey. With this information, we aim to answer questions raised by you, the VAS Workshop Applicants and Alumni, in order to produce reports that we’ll share with you. These will:

  • • Help you understand who your peers’ trusted VAS providers are, especially those with a track record of M4D VAS
  • • Help you understand potential solutions to the challenges of implementing M4D VAS
  • • Increase your understanding of how your company’s approach to VAS compares to that of other operators
